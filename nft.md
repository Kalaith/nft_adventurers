Game Concept: NFT Adventurers - Dungeons & Dragons Idle Adventure

Your game sounds like a perfect blend of classic D&D tabletop vibes with blockchain ownership and idle progression. Players own Adventurer NFTs (heroes with unique traits, classes, and levels), send them on timed missions into procedurally generated dungeons or quests, earn Loot NFTs (gear, artifacts), and even tame Wild Monsters as Pet NFTs. Everything is fully on-chain for true ownership, tradable on a marketplace, and upgradeable via smart contracts. It's "idle RPG meets play-to-earn" – log in daily to check results, manage your roster, and scale your empire.

Core Gameplay Loop
Build Your Party: Collect or mint Adventurer NFTs. Equip them with Loot NFTs for bonuses.
Send on Missions: Assign parties (3-5 adventurers + 1-2 pets) to quests. Missions last 1-48 hours, with success based on party stats vs. difficulty + random Chainlink VRF dice rolls (D&D style!).
Collect Rewards: XP levels up NFTs (metadata updates), Loot drops, gold/tokens, or tamed pets. Failures risk injuries, gear loss, or (optionally) permadeath.
Progress & Trade: Fuse/breed for rarer NFTs, stake for passive yields, compete in guilds or PvP arenas.
This draws inspiration from games like Illuvium (creature taming and auto-battles) and The Beacon (dungeon loot hunts with NFTs), but emphasizes D&D classes, party synergy, and idle delegation.

function generateFeat(nftId, enemyType, killCount, perilLevel, vrfSeed):
  basePrefix = hash(enemyType + vrfSeed) % prefixPool  // e.g., "Gob-", "Drag-", "God-"
  tier = min(killCount / 10, perilLevel)  // 0=Common, 10+=Mythic
  suffix = ["Bane", "Piercer", "Vanquisher", "Slayer"][tier % 4]
  name = basePrefix + suffix  // "Thor-Godslayer"
  bonuses = {dmgVsType: tier*10%, auraRadius: tier*5}  // Procedural stats
  appendToMetadata(nftId, {feat: name, bonuses, timestamp, proof: vrfProof})
